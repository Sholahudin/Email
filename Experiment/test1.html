PEOPLE AT YOUR SERVICE

Thierry Helterlin

Thierry joined the LOHR Service team in 2001 as an electrician, while working on the continuous improvement of the workstations in the workshop.

In 2012, in order to ensure the implementation and monitoring of production, Thierry worked in our subsidiary company in India for 6 months. Once back, he had the position of workshop team-leader for 3 years.

He is in charge of the LOHR Service Duppigheim workshop since 4 years now. Thierry is in charge of 35 people, consisted of 2 teams. He also ensures billings, quality and work monitoring. In the meantime, Thierry also had the opportunity to monitor the quality of our subsidiary company in Turkey.

For Thierry, LOHR is a company on a human scale where we know how to put people and their skills to the fore. What he likes is that LOHR Service brings together all the skills of LOHR and that they are put at the service of the customer. The priority is to ensure quality service and customer satisfaction. He has, to quote him, "pride in our product and the service we can provide to the customer".
